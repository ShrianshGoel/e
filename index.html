Reason for building this website ?

I have built this website for the cause of women empowerment in Mussoorie (a town in Uttarakhand) . As a resident of Uttarakhand , I feel very sad that the literacy level in Uttarakhand is far below the national average. The percentages displayed on the governmentâ€™s website is quite low and I believe that a copious number of cases of violation of right to education remains unreported. There are numerous instances in Uttarakhand where parents have been against the decision of giving their girl child the right to education. When I interacted with girls from various age groups residing in the sub-urban areas of Mussoorie , I got to know that many girls have not got the right to education. The reason for building the website was my determination to eradicate illiteracy in Mussoorie. Through this website we would try raising substantial funds so that we could provide girls with the right to education and raise awareness among parents who fail to treat their girl child equally and have a general bias for the boy child . Also , there will be a database created using this website so that we could report the data to the concerned authorities.
